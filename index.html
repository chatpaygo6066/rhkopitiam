Our story

Miri's favourite
kopitiam

RH Kopitiam is where Miri comes to eat. Nestled inside Miri Times Square at Marina Parkcity, we serve authentic Malay and Malaysian favourites — from the aromatic depths of Sarawak Laksa to the comforting simplicity of Roti Kawin with a glass of pulled Teh Tarik.

We're open daily from 7AM — whether you're here for breakfast before work, a family lunch, or a satisfying dinner after a long day. Our generous portions and honest prices keep our regulars coming back every single day.

Fully halal-certified, wheelchair-friendly, and spacious enough for the whole family.
